About SifMA

SifMA (Smart Farming Monitor & Automasi) simulates an integrated smart farming system.

Parameters:

  • Air Humidity: Ideal is 40-70%.
  • Soil Moisture: Ideal is 30-80%. Water when it gets low!
  • Light: The system simulates a day/night cycle.

Controls:

  • Manual Buttons: Directly turn the Sprinkler or Grow Light on/off.
  • Auto Toggles: Let the system manage things for you.
    • Auto Sprinkler: Turns on when soil is too dry.
    • Auto Light: Turns on when it gets dark.

Warnings will appear in the log when conditions are not optimal. Pay attention to them to save your plant!
